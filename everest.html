Overview

The Everest Base Camp Trek is one of the world's most iconic adventures, taking you through the heart of the Khumbu region to the foot of the world's highest mountain. This legendary journey offers breathtaking Himalayan panoramas, rich Sherpa culture, and a profound sense of accomplishment.

Highlights

Spectacular views of Everest, Lhotse, Nuptse, and Ama Dablam
Explore ancient monasteries and Buddhist culture
Challenge yourself on the world's most famous trek
Stay in traditional teahouses with Sherpa hospitality

Gallery

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu (1,400m)
Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport, transfer to hotel, trek briefing and preparation.
Day 2: Fly to Lukla (2,860m), trek to Phakding (2,610m)
Spectacular mountain flight to Lukla, begin trek through Sherpa villages.
Day 3: Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m)
Cross suspension bridges, enter Sagarmatha National Park, first Everest views.
Day 4: Acclimatization day in Namche
Hike to Everest View Hotel, visit Sherpa Museum and local market.
Day 5-11: Trek to Base Camp and return
Detailed itinerary available upon booking.
Day 12: Fly back to Kathmandu
Celebration dinner and cultural show.
